What is AVENTON?

Aventon's story is simple - less talk, more do! Aventon concentrates on superior quality, better attention to detail, and hands-on manufacturing. Set up in 2013, JW (Aventon's Creator) built an entry-level fixed frame with a third-party supplier. A year later, unhappy with their work, he set up his own factory, and today, with over 100 employees, Aventon builds brilliant Single Speed/Fixed, Gravel, and E-bikes. Always innovating and creating bikes that are accessible and affordable, Aventon is the go-to brand for riders who want something different. We stock the full range of their fixed/single-speed bikes as well as the Kijote gravel bike and other Aventon spare parts.
